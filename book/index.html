Mark A. Miller

Planetary Climate Researcher

Modeling Planetary Climates with Python is a tutorial introduction to the principles and methods of simple planetary and paleo climate modeling. It is built on a foundation of real Earth and planetary observations, fundamental principles of physics, chemistry, and mathematics, as well the unprecedented ease of programming in Python.

The outline is very tentative, and will almost certainly change. This page will be updated as work on the book proceeds. Please contact me if you have any helpful feedback.
